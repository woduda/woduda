## 🧭 How I work

- **Long-lived systems over greenfield hype.** Modernizing legacy codebases without service interruption is a craft I've practiced for a decade.

- **Domain literacy first.** Factoring, e-invoicing, regulatory compliance (JPK, VAT White List, Split Payment, GIIF/AML) — I learn the business before I touch the architecture.
- **Bridge between business and engineering.** I translate stakeholder requirements into systems, and engineering trade-offs back into business language.
